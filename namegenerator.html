About Quar Names

The quar names used in this generator are sourced from a variety of sources, including: Western Iron, the ZombieSmith website, and documentation regarding "Quar: Battle for Gate 18."

Quar naming conventions can vary, but tend to lean towards the goofy side. No clear naming convention has been determined by the developers of this app that indicate the nationality or gender of a quar, therefore this generator compiles names without regard to either detail. Go with what feels right and makes the most sense to you!

We hope this generator will help inspire you while naming your quar. Be sure to share what you create!
